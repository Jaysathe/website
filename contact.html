BugX is a leading cybersecurity company providing enterprise-grade security solutions including penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, red team assessments, and more.

Our assessments combine both automated tools and manual expert testing to provide comprehensive coverage that finds vulnerabilities automation might miss.

Yes, we support integration with a wide range of enterprise systems, CI/CD pipelines, cloud platforms, and security tools.
Is BugX secure for enterprise use?
Absolutely. We follow strict security protocols, comply with major industry standards, and prioritize the confidentiality and security of your data.
How quickly can BugX be implemented?
Implementation timelines vary based on scope, but many of our assessments can begin within 1-2 weeks of initial contact.
